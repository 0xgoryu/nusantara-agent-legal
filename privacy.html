This Privacy Policy explains how Nusantara Recipe Agent collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application. We are committed to full transparency about our data practices.
Section 01

Who We Are

Nusantara Recipe Agent is an automated content tool that creates and publishes Indonesian recipe videos to TikTok. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Application.

Contact: arridloriziq@gmail.com
Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to operate the Application:

Data TypePurposeRetention
TikTok Access Token Required to publish videos to your TikTok account via the API Until revoked or expired
TikTok Open ID Identifies which TikTok account to post to Until revoked
TikTok Refresh Token Renews access token automatically without re-authentication Until revoked or expired (365 days)

We do not collect your TikTok password, personal profile information, follower data, messages, or any data beyond what is listed above.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating with the TikTok API on your behalf
  • Publishing AI-generated recipe video content to your TikTok account
  • Maintaining continuous posting access via token refresh

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or any purpose other than the core posting functionality described above.

Section 04

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. The Application communicates with the following third-party services solely to generate and publish content:

ServicePurposePrivacy Policy
TikTok API Video publishing tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
OpenRouter AI recipe generation openrouter.ai/privacy
Together.ai AI food image generation together.ai/privacy
ElevenLabs Indonesian voiceover generation elevenlabs.io/privacy
Creatomate Video rendering creatomate.com/privacy

None of your TikTok credentials are shared with any of the above services. Only the content assets (images, audio, video) are processed by these services.

Section 05

Data Storage and Security

  • TikTok tokens are stored in secured server environment variables and are never exposed in source code or public repositories
  • We do not maintain a user database or any backend storing your personal information
  • All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Generated video files are temporary and deleted from our servers after successful upload to TikTok
Section 06

Your Rights and Controls

You have full control over this Application's access to your TikTok account:

  • Revoke access: Go to TikTok → Settings → Security → Authorized Apps → remove Nusantara Recipe Agent at any time
  • Data deletion: Revoking access immediately invalidates all tokens. No residual data is retained.
  • Content removal: Any videos posted to your TikTok account can be deleted directly from TikTok at any time
Section 07

Children's Privacy

This Application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has used this Application, please contact us immediately.
