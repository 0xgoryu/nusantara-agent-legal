FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how Nusantara Food Agent works.

Nusantara Food Agent is an automated content tool that generates traditional Indonesian recipe videos and publishes them to TikTok automatically. It runs a full AI pipeline — recipe writing, food image generation, Indonesian voiceover, and video rendering — then posts the finished video to your connected TikTok account up to 3 times per day.
The app requests three scopes: user.info.basic to identify your TikTok account, video.upload to upload the video file, and video.publish to publish the video as a post. We do not access your followers, messages, liked videos, or any other account data.
You can revoke access at any time directly from TikTok:

On mobile: TikTok app → Profile → Menu (☰) → Settings → Security → Authorized Apps → find Nusantara Food Agent → Remove access.

On desktop: tiktok.com → Profile → Settings → Security → Manage account access → Revoke.

Your Rights

Data & Privacy Controls

You are in full control of your data and the app's access to your TikTok account.

👁️

Right to Know

We only store your TikTok access token and open ID. Nothing else. Read our full Privacy Policy.

🗑️

Right to Delete

Revoke app access from TikTok settings and all tokens are immediately invalidated and deleted.

🚫

Right to Opt Out

Stop the app from posting at any time by revoking access or stopping the scheduler on your server.

✉️

Right to Contact

For any data request or concern, email us and we will respond within 7 business days.

How to Revoke TikTok Access

Remove Nusantara Food Agent's access to your TikTok account in three steps:

  • Open TikTok app → go to Profile → tap the menu icon (☰) in the top right
  • Go to Settings → Security → Authorized Apps
  • Find "Nusantara Food Agent" → tap Remove Access
