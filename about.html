📖 About

Preserving Indonesian
Culinary Heritage

Nusantara Food Agent uses artificial intelligence to make authentic Indonesian recipes accessible to the world through daily TikTok content.

Our Mission

Why We Built This

"Indonesia has over 5,000 traditional recipes across its 17,000 islands. Nusantara Food Agent exists to document, celebrate, and share this culinary heritage — one recipe video at a time."

The Problem

Traditional Indonesian recipes — passed down through generations — are being forgotten as younger audiences turn to fast food and western cuisine. Quality food content in Bahasa Indonesia remains scarce on short-form video platforms.

Our Solution

An AI-powered automation pipeline that generates authentic recipe content daily, complete with professional visuals, natural Indonesian voiceover, and engaging video format — reaching new audiences on TikTok consistently.

Platform Integration

TikTok API Usage

Nusantara Food Agent integrates with TikTok through the official TikTok for Developers platform.

How We Use TikTok's API

The application uses Login Kit to authenticate the account owner via OAuth 2.0 — a one-time authorization that grants permission to post content on their behalf.

The Content Posting API is used exclusively to upload and publish AI-generated recipe videos. The app posts automatically at scheduled times using the video.upload and video.publish scopes.

User identity verification uses user.info.basic only to confirm the correct TikTok account is being posted to. No profile data, follower information, or private content is accessed.

Users may revoke access at any time via TikTok Settings → Security → Authorized Apps.
